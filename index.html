Problem

Tenders are often lost in the detail long before adjudication.

Most pricing failures are not dramatic. They are small omissions, optimistic assumptions, weak exclusions, underestimated prelims, and rates that do not hold under delivery pressure.

Typical pricing failures

  • Missing BOQ items or undermeasured scope
    Especially where revisions, addenda, or scope splits were not fully integrated.
  • Rates built on weak assumptions
    Labour outputs, plant utilisation, productivity, wastage, logistics, and escalation treated too lightly.
  • Exclusions that do not protect the contractor
    Leaving room for disputes, rework, or silent scope transfer after award.
  • Margin exposed by commercial blind spots
    Discounts, contingencies, prelims, and risk allowances not matching the actual tender environment.

Why an independent review matters

Estimating teams work under pressure. Internal familiarity can hide weak assumptions because everyone is already close to the job. A second review changes the question from “did we price it?” to “did we price it properly?”.

ClearTender is designed for that exact gap: a practical pre-submission review that improves clarity, protects margin, and gives decision-makers a stronger basis for sign-off.

FAQ

Questions contractors usually ask before sending a tender pack.

Who is this for?

ClearTender is for South African contractors pricing building and civil work who want an independent commercial review before submission.

Does this replace my estimator or QS?

No. ClearTender is a second commercial review, not a replacement for your internal pricing function.

Do you provide engineering signoff or certification?

No. ClearTender does not provide engineering signoff, QS certification, or legal advice.

What documents should be included?

At minimum: BOQ, pricing summary, tender instructions, major assumptions, exclusions, and any revision-sensitive drawings or addenda affecting cost.

Can the review be done close to deadline?

Yes, subject to availability and the complexity of the tender pack. The clearer the submission documents, the more useful the turnaround.

Will you tell me what to price?

No. ClearTender reviews the commercial logic and visible risk in your pricing. Final pricing responsibility remains with the contractor.
